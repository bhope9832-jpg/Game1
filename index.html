🦴 RigStudio 3D

Rig and animate 3D characters right on your phone.

  • Build — drag joints to shape the skeleton. ➕ Add Bone: tap the model to drop a joint — it snaps & auto-connects to the closest bone. Drag a loose joint near another to connect them.
  • 🧲 Bind Skin — attaches the mesh to your bones automatically.
  • Pose — drag a joint to rotate its bone, or use the sliders for exact control. 🙂 opens face expressions; ⚡ toggles jiggle physics (tails, ears…).
  • Animate — pose, press ◆ to keyframe, move the playhead, pose again, then ▶ to play.
  • 1 finger orbits, 2 fingers pan/zoom. Tap 🦴 for the bone list.
  • ☰ menu: new human/animal characters, import your own .glb/.gltf/.obj model (✨ auto-places the skeleton for you), save/load.
